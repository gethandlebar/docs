This is where Handlebar becomes your compliance team's favorite tool.

## Event Immutability

Every tool call, rule evaluation, and action is logged immutably. 

This isn't just good practice. In regulated industries, it's a requirement.

## Evidence Extraction

Handlebar makes it easy to pull evidence for:
- Internal audits
- Regulatory inquiries (HIPAA, SOC2, etc.)
- Incident investigations

You can filter by:
- Time range
- Agent
- Tool
- Action taken (e.g., "show me all blocked tool calls")
- Rule triggered

Export formats include JSON, CSV, and structured compliance reports.

## Regulatory Mapping

We're building explicit mappings to common compliance frameworks:
- **SOC2**: Access controls, audit logging, change management
- **Internal audits**: Custom rule sets, custom reporting

This is still in progress, but the foundation is there. And it's designed to extend.
